About DuitPintar

Welcome to DuitPintar, a personal finance and investing blog created to help everyday people make smarter money decisions.

We believe financial literacy shouldn't be complicated or boring. Our mission is to break down complex topics into simple, actionable advice that you can apply immediately — whether you're just starting to save or already managing a portfolio.

DuitPintar was founded in 2025 by a team of finance enthusiasts who got tired of seeing friends and family struggle with money. We're not certified financial advisors, but we're dedicated researchers who translate expert knowledge into plain English (and Indonesian!).

If you have questions, suggestions, or just want to say hi, visit our Contact page.

— The DuitPintar Team
